About DCMS

The Digital Complaint Management & Tracking System is a professional grievance redressal platform designed for citizens, students, employees and customers. It modernizes the way complaints are filed, routed and resolved.

Objectives
  • Provide a single online channel for grievance intake
  • Bring full transparency to status and resolution
  • Empower departments with triage, assignment and analytics
Technologies

Flask · SQLAlchemy · SQLite · Bootstrap 5 · Chart.js · ReportLab · Pandas · OpenPyXL · QR Code.
